# Common Support Topics

## Playback and timers

If sounds or timers do not behave as expected, restart the app and confirm your device allows audio playback for Lullby.

## Presets and shared links

Shared preset links should open Lullby when the app is installed. If a link opens on the web first, use the open button on the preset page.

## Premium features

Purchases and subscriptions are managed by the App Store or Google Play. Use your platform subscription settings for billing, renewal, and cancellation controls.

## Privacy requests

For privacy or deletion requests, email us and include enough detail to identify the account, preset, or shared content involved.

# Response Time

We try to respond as soon as possible. Response times may vary while Lullby is in active development.
