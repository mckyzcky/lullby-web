# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 18, 2026

Welcome to Lullby.

Lullby is a premium ambient sound app designed to help users create relaxing soundscapes, save presets, and enjoy immersive visual scenes. We are committed to protecting your privacy and collecting as little personal information as possible.

---

# 1. Data We Collect

## A. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Lullby stores the following information locally on your device:

- Sound volume preferences
- Active sound selections
- Saved presets
- Favorite presets
- Default timer duration
- Background playback setting
- AI mood suggestion setting
- Gyro parallax setting
- Theme preference
- Display name
- Listening statistics, including:
    - Total listening time
    - Daily listening time
    - Sound usage statistics

This information is used solely to personalize your experience.

---

## B. Data Stored in the Cloud

If you choose to use synced presets or community features, Lullby may store:

- Preset name
- Preset description
- Sound configuration
- Preset visibility setting
- Preset source
- Creation timestamp
- Anonymous creator identifier

---

## C. Automatically Collected Technical Data

Lullby may collect limited technical information such as:

- App version
- Device type
- Operating system version
- Crash reports
- Performance diagnostics
- Feature usage statistics

This information is used to improve reliability, stability, and user experience.

---

## D. Location Used for Weather Suggestions

If AI mood suggestions are enabled and you grant location permission, Lullby may access your current location to request local weather information for weather-aware preset suggestions.

Your location is used only for that weather lookup. Lullby does not store your precise location, does not store location history, and does not use location in the background.

The weather lookup may send latitude and longitude to Open-Meteo to return current weather conditions.

---

## E. Data We Do Not Collect

Lullby does **not** collect:

- Microphone recordings
- Voice recordings
- Audio from your surroundings
- Contacts
- Photos
- Videos
- Messages
- Health data
- Location history
- Payment card details
- Real name
- Email address

unless explicitly required by a future feature and disclosed separately.

---

# 2. Anonymous Accounts

Lullby may create an anonymous account identifier to support:

- Cloud synchronization
- Community presets
- Preset ownership

This identifier is not intended to personally identify you.

---

# 3. Community Presets

If you publish presets to the community, other users may be able to view:

- Preset name
- Preset description
- Sound configuration

Please do not include personal, confidential, or sensitive information in community content.

Lullby reserves the right to remove content that violates our Terms of Service.

---

# 4. Local Storage

Lullby stores preferences and settings on your device using local storage technologies.

Data remains on your device until:

- You delete it
- You reset the application
- You uninstall the app

---

# 5. Audio and Microphone

Lullby only plays ambient sounds selected by you.

Lullby does not:

- Record audio
- Access microphone recordings
- Analyze conversations
- Upload audio captured from your device

---

# 6. Motion Sensors

If Gyro Parallax is enabled, Lullby may use device motion sensor data to create visual movement effects.

Motion data:

- Remains on your device
- Is processed locally
- Is not transmitted to Lullby servers

---

# 7. Background Playback

If enabled, Lullby may continue playing ambient audio while the app is in the background.

Lullby does not track activity performed outside the app.

---

# 8. Third-Party Services

Lullby may use trusted third-party services including:

- Supabase
- Apple App Store
- Google Play Store
- Open-Meteo
- Analytics providers
- Crash reporting providers

These services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

---

# 9. Payments and Subscriptions

If Lullby offers premium features, purchases are processed through:

- Apple App Store
- Google Play Store

Lullby does not receive or store payment card information.

---

# 10. Data Retention

### Local Data

Data stored on your device remains until removed by you.

### Cloud Data

Community presets and synced presets may remain stored until:

- Deleted by you
- Removed due to policy violations
- Removed during maintenance or service changes

---

# 11. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect stored information.

However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

# 12. Your Choices

You may:

- Delete saved presets
- Disable background playback
- Disable AI mood suggestions
- Disable gyro parallax
- Deny or revoke location permission
- Change your display name
- Remove community presets you created
- Uninstall the app to remove local data

Where technically possible, you may also request deletion of cloud-stored preset data associated with your anonymous account.

---

# 13. Children's Privacy

Lullby is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided information to Lullby, please contact us.

---

# 14. International Users

Your information may be processed in countries where Lullby or its service providers operate.

By using Lullby, you understand and agree that information may be transferred and processed outside your country of residence.

---

# 15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Updated versions will be made available within the app or through our website.

Continued use of Lullby after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
